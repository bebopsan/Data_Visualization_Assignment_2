Visualization of relational data

About the data

This data visualization example is based on publications from the postdeadline sessions (Th5A, Th5B, Th5C) of the Optical Fiber Communications (OFC) conference held on 2016.

Postdeadline submissions are not an opportunity to submit a paper after the published deadline, but are intended to provide those with late-breaking work an opportunity to submit that work for consideration. Postdeadline submissions should describe new and significant material in rapidly advancing areas. A limited number of postdeadline submissions will be selected for presentation, and only those papers judged to be truly excellent and compelling in their timeliness will be accepted [1] .

The idea is to visualize the impact of the high quality research from the number of citations, in an interactive way using circles. The size of the circles indicates the numer of times that the procedings paper has been cited, and the group to which the circle belongs indicates the session. More information on the paper can be obtained on demand by hovering the mouse over the circle or clicking on it.

Basic use instructions

  • Hover over the circles to get a tooltip with key information about each paper.
  • Hold Ctr and click on a circle to get redirected to the Url of OSA where the file can be downloaded.
